Published on: 2024-12-21T12:12:00Z

Summary: I have a lot of Linux boxes, mostly debian 10-11-12, where, on some of them (Id say about 20% of them) the networking activity stops at fixed intervals (usually 45s). By "stops" I mean rx/tx is exactly as the previous second: cat /sys/class/net/eno1/statistics/rx_bytes cat /sys/class/net/eno1/statistics/tx_bytes I only noticed this after implementing a monitor due to some complaints I had. Now, I cant find the culprit. I tried making a delta of processes suspecting something like a cron but I have nothing. Cron is disabled, I dont see anything suspect. Where do you suggest I should be looking so I can detect whatever is causing this?
