Cannot fg a zsh function including less

Published on: 2024-12-19T03:43:00Z

Summary: I have this zsh function: f() { /usr/bin/find . -type f | less } When I run this function, suspend with Ctrl+z, and then run fg, it displays zsh: suspended (tty output) /usr/bin/find . -type f | and I cant resume. Why is that? How can I resume? arch% f zsh: suspended /usr/bin/find . -type f | arch% fg [2] - continued /usr/bin/find . -type f | zsh: suspended (tty output) /usr/bin/find . -type f | zsh: suspended (tty output) /usr/bin/find . -type f | arch% If I run /usr/bin/find . -type f | less directly from the command line without making it a function, it will suspend and resume normally.
