How safe are password generator sites for htaccess

Published on: 2024-12-27T17:05:07Z

Summary: I wish to create an encrypted password for an .htaccess file. All tutorials that Ive seen suggest that I use a site that will encrypt my password for me. But it seems to me that this is inherently risky, because the sites that offer this service could theoretically keep the information that I supply – and the password that they generate for me – and use it to gain access to the very portions of my site that I am seeking to protect. They wouldnt automatically know which URLs and/or folders that I was protecting, but I imagine they could figure that out in at least some cases, if they felt motivated to do so. So my questions are twofold: Is my concern misplaced? If not, can someone recommend a password creation site that is recognized as trustworthy?
